Ohm's Law Calculator

This free electrical engineering calculator helps you calculate Voltage, Current, Resistance, and Power using Ohm’s Law. It is useful for diploma students, BTech students, and professionals.

Formulas:

  • V = I × R
  • I = V / R
  • R = V / I
  • P = V × I

Example:

If Voltage = 10V and Current = 2A, then Resistance = 5Ω and Power = 20W.
